#Built-For-Efficiency

Self-priming close-coupled centrifugal pumps with built-in ejector system, designed to remain primed even in the presence of water-dissolved gases.
The extensive use of pressed stainless steel ensures a high-performance, durable and lightweight pump.
Suitable for use with drinking water.

Pump Features

  • Quiet operation due to the anti vibration system between shaft and impeller
  • More efficient motor with low energy consumption and lower operating temperature.
  • Special paint which gives improved resistance against environmental corrosion.
  • Longer lifespan
  • More compact
  • Application areas are:
    • Water treatment systems
    • HVAC systems
    • Industrial boosting
    • Water supply systems
  • The motor is a fully enclosed, air-cooled two pole standard motor

Motor Features

  • Pumps are close coupled to a TEFC brushless induction motor designed for continuous operation.
  • A thermal relay is provided in the windings to protect against electrical and mechanical overload and the pump can be connected directly to the mains power supply through a 10amp fuse or MCB.
