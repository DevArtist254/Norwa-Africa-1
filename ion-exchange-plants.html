Ion Exchange Plant

Effectively removing contaminants from liquids, providing successful separation, and efficiently catalyzing reactions

Our ion exchange plants are used in water treatment, including water softening, industrial demineralization, condensate polishing, ultrapure water production, and wastewater treatment. It can also provide a method of separation in many nonwater processes, such as desiccation and chromatographic separation. It has special utility in chemical synthesis, manufacturing, food processing, mining, power generation, agriculture, and a variety of applications and other industries.

Feed water quality

  • Raw water appearance: clear
  • Temperature range: 5 - 10 degrees
  • Iron: < 0.1 ppm
  • Min inlet pressure: 2 bar
  • Chlorine: < 0.5 ppm
  • Max operating pressure: 6 bar
