Destory Bacteria & Viruses In Water With UV

The Four filter system is an energy saving, environmentally friendly, small size and easy operation water treatment machine with an Ultra-Violet system that destorys bacteria and contaminants.

Basic Features

  • Water Flow : 1.5m/Min
  • Filter Type : Simple Filter with PP or Block Carbon
  • Dimensions : 500*300*360mm
  • Certified for use in potable water.

Product Features

  • Small size and Easy operation
  • The single filter can either:
    • Sediment filter to remove dirt, rust other solid objects or
    • Carbon filter that takes out 98% of the chlorine and organic chemicals, herbicides, pesticides or,
    • Block activated carbon a fine sediment reduction for removal of odor, taste, cyst sand turbidity.
  • Ultraviolet Sterilizer : Destory bacteria & viruses in water with UV rays
  • Energy saving, environment protection.
