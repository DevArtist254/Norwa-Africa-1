Kleen MCT511E

It's a high pH liquid formulation designed to remove organics, silt, and other particulate deposits from polysulfone, fluorocarbon, and all polyamide thin film composite reverse osmosis (RO) and ultrafiltration (UF) membranes.

Kleen MCT882

It's a low pH, liquid formulation, specifically designed to remove calcium carbonate and other inorganic scales from reverse osmosis (RO), nanofiltration (NF), and ultrafiltration (UF) membranes. Kleen MCT882 can also remove organic foulants from the membrane surface.

Citric acid

Low pH membrane cleaner.

Caustic soda

High pH cleaner and used of removal of biofouling in membranes and silica fouling.

Antiscalant

Prevent silica fouling of membranes. Hyperseperse(suitable for high and low silica base on dilution). Low silica antiscalant. High silica antiscalant.

Sodium Metabisulphate (SMBS)

Is used widely for preservation of reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration membranes. It's also for dichlorination.
