Stainless steel needle Valve

Our stainless steel needle Valve is a compact 2-way stainless steel needle valve with 1/2" - 1 1/4" threads. This valve can be used as a reliable shut-off valve, or to accurately control (relative low) flow rates of clean gases or fluids. The valve has a nominal internal diameter of 4.8 mm. The housing is made of stainless steel 316 and the seals are made of PTFE. The max. pressure is 300 bar and the temperature range is from -20 to max. 200 Â°C. Thanks to the high quality materials and robust construction, the valve is suited for high pressures and agressive fluids.
