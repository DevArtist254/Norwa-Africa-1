Our Brand Story

Norwa Africa was established with the purpose to provide reliable water treatment and water pumping solutions in Africa. We endeavour to address the water scarcity, the challenging water quality as well as the growing demand for green energy through solar solutions and water treatment machines. Since its establishment, Norwa Africa has been on a journey, seeking solutions in water pumping, solar power, water treatment for African communities and governments. With the growing water scarcity, need for renewable energy, and the numerous water quality concerns in Africa, Norwa Africa dedicates itself to provide reliable water treatment solutions in the most professional approach, to uplift our communities. We are proud of its success and look to the future with enthusiasm, to grow with the needs of our customers, to transform communities and change lives.

How We Do It

Engineering

Norwa Africa's engineering team is well versed with the water treatment and green energy needs and issues across Africa. Our company has designed and executed numerous water treatment projects across the region. Norwa Africa's process involves the need analysis, scoping, system design, costing, cost-benefit analysis, installation & construction, and finally operation and maintenance.

Procurement

A good water treatment project is largely dependent on great design and quality products. Norwa Africa selects the most optimal equipment from the top manufacturers across the world. At all times, the primary focus is on ensuring the right quality, effectiveness and efficiency for our customers.

Construction

Norwa Africa's technical teams include highly experienced engineers and technicians. They employ cutting-edge technologies with the objective of building and installing the highest quality projects. They also run operation and maintenance programs for all our water treatment projects.
