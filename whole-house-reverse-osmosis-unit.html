The Treatment Process

This machine uses Reverse Osmosis to purify water with a larger output of 400 gallons per day(1500 liters per day) . Reverse Osmosis is a treatment process for the removal of all dissolved solids and bacteria to provide totally pure water for small-scale and large application.

Basic Features

  • Water flow 1500 liters per day
  • Pressure tank : 11g plastic tank
  • Power : 100~240V, 50/60HZ, any power plug
  • Meter\digital display and pressure gauge
  • Filter type : simple filter with PP or block carbon
  • Housing: 122-20 single "o"ring housing
