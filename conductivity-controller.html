TDS & conductivity controller

Total Dissolved Solids(TDS) are the total amount of mobile charged ions,including salts or metals dissolved in a given volume of water. TDS,which is based on conductivity,is expressed in parts per million(ppm) or milligrams per liter(mg/L).TDS includes any inorganic element present other than the pure water molecule(H2O) and suspended solids.
