Applying pressure-driving purification to meet stringent water-quality standards.

Ultrafiltration (UF) plant uses pressure-driven purification process that separates particulate matter from soluble compounds using an ultrafine membrane media. Our UF plants are an excellent separation technology for desalination pretreatment, reverse osmosis pretreatment, and wastewater reclamation, as well as for producing potable water.
With over years of separation-technology leadership and ultrafiltration plant installations in Africa, we offer a portfolio of designed plants for outstanding membrane separation, extreme productivity and efficiency, and exceptional reliability.

Raw water quality

  • Max operating tempture: 40 degrees
  • Recovery range: 70 - 98% depending on the raw water quality
